TweetHit

Affiliate Linking


It is possible to direct people to TweetHit using unique URLs, so that when a user buys a product, the whole comission is transferred to your Amazon Associates account.

Step 1


Create affiliate accounts for Amazon Stores
(You should provide a website that you own)

Amazon.com: affiliate-program.amazon.com
Amazon.co.uk: affiliate-program.amazon.co.uk
Amazon.ca: associates.amazon.ca
Amazon.de: partnernet.amazon.de
Amazon.fr: partenaires.amazon.fr
Amazon.jp: affiliate.amazon.co.jp

Tip: You can first register for Amazon.co.uk and then activate associate accounts for both Amazon.de and Amazon.fr within Amazon.co.uk settings page.

Step 2


Go the Affiliate Settings screen (Need Google account for login)
Save your affiliate IDs
Get your unique referral URL

Step 3


Direct traffic to TweetHit using your unique URL.
All of the affiliate IDs will be replaced for your unique URL

Click here to see it in action: UNIQUE URL
The associate ID suffixes for every product will be 'tweethit-test'
For example:
(http://www.amazon.com/o/asin/asdlkf/tweethit-test)
